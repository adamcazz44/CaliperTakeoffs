Pricing

How pricing works

No subscription, no retainer, no per-seat software you have to learn. You send a set, I send a number, and you decide.

Quoted per job

Priced off the actual set — how many sheets, which trades are in scope, and how much detail the drawings carry. A 40-sheet renovation and a 400-sheet ground-up aren't the same job, so they aren't the same price.

Confirmed before I start

You get the scope, the turnaround, and the price up front, in writing. Nothing begins until you've agreed to all three. The meter is never running while you decide.

What's excluded is written down

Every takeoff ships with Exclusions and Clarifications tabs alongside the numbers. You know exactly what was counted and what wasn't — before you price it, not after you've bid it.

Who does the work

Case by case. If a revision means starting the takeoff over, there will likely be a fee — and I'll confirm it with you before I redo any work.
